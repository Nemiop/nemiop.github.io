create a quadcopter

Quadcopters burst into our life with technological progress. Let's make a quadrocopter by our hands! It's cheaper than buying the finished model in the store. And much more exciting!

The main idea of the project is to create an inexpensive quadcopter that has a self-powered and algorithm for flight stabilization on the Arduino. The cost of this Quad is approximately equal to 60 $.The main thing is the desire to make the quadcopter with your hands :)

Robots in our life

Each of our visitors desires his own robot and now is final. We give you a chance to create special model of robot from your dream!

On the Arduino it is very easy to make different cars with remote control, simple sensors and logic. Almost all the questions that you have in the process of development of robots on Arduino, someone already asked, and you can find the answers here :)

How to built a tank

RC tank is the dream of not only boys, but many men of all ages. Easier, of course, to purchase a RC toy, which is filled with shops. But, applying knowledge of electronics, you can make a RC model tank based on the regular design team.

Until recently, the creation of robots required the developer of high qualification. But with the advent of Arduino boards, this lesson may afford almost anyone who is even slightly familiar with the programming! Easy, but everything in order :)
